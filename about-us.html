How does it work

As a process transformation company, we rethinks and rebuilds processes for the digital

01. Understanding Client Requirements

Discuss the client's hiring needs, organizational goals, job roles, and desired candidate profile.

02. Talent Sourcing

Post job advertisements on career websites, LinkedIn, and other professional networks.

03. Screening & Shortlisting

Assess candidate resumes to match qualifications, skills, and experience with the job requirements.

04. Interview Process

Conduct behavioral and technical interviews to assess expertise and alignment with company values.

05. Reference Checks

Contact references provided by candidates to verify their previous work experience, performance, and character.

06. Offer & Negotiation

Draft and extend job offers, outlining compensation, benefits, and job responsibilities.

07. Onboarding Support

Assist with documentation, compliance, and setting up the candidate in their new role.

08. Post-Hire Follow-Up

Check in with both the client and the new hire after the probation period to ensure satisfaction.
